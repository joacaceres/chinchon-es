  • Cards game for 2 to 4 players
  • The objective is to finish the game with the lowest amount of points
  • Each round starts with 7 cards for each player and face up on the table
  • The player at the right starts picking up a card, always pick a card to start your turn and droping one to finish it
  • To win you have to make combos of 3 or 4 cards, of the same number or consecutive numbers and same kind, the remaining cards will be your points
  • You can score minus 10 points if one of your combos has 4 cards and you end up the round with no spare cards
  • To finish the round you need to have 2 combos or chinchon(7 consecutive cards) and put a card that is equal or less than 5 upside down
  • At the end of the round, the cards without combos are counted as points (spare joker scores 25) and added to the players
  • If someone scores 70 or 100(as desired) the game ends for that person and the rest continue
